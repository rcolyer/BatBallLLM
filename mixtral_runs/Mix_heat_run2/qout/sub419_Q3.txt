Around 75%, based on experience and an assumption about general human ability and willingness to consider more than just face value information presented to them. But, like everything, it will vary person by person and task to task, some might consider it or remember examples where most of the participants got the question wrong while others will only focus on times when everyone around them solved problems similar to it correctly.
